My first time cooking: a hot pan, a sprinkle too much salt, and nerves all around. It wasn’t perfect, but it was mine. That day I realized cooking isn’t about perfection—it’s about trying, learning, and enjoying every messy, delicious moment.

#01

Be prepared


Always read the recipe and prepare all needed ingredients. The ingredients are 90% of the success.




#02

Choose the right recipe


There are millions of recipes on the internet. Always read the comments below the article.




#03

Taste As You Go


Don’t wait until the end to season. Taste frequently and adjust spices gradually.




#04

Embrace Mistakes


Burnt edges or extra salt? That’s how you learn. Each mistake is a step toward better cooking.




#05

Enjoy the Process


Cooking is not just about the food—it’s about experimenting, creating, and enjoying the journey
